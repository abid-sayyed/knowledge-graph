On February 10, 2023, Apple released a new product called VisionNext.

Tim Cook introduced VisionNext during the annual Apple Event in California.

VisionNext integrates with Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud services.

The system offers:

* adaptive display rendering
* spatial interaction control
* low-power neural processing
* encrypted collaboration tools

Developers from Meta Platforms also contributed to compatibility testing.

Mark Zuckerberg confirmed the partnership in April 2023.

VisionNext entered public beta in August 2023.
