Artificial Intelligence Collaboration Study

Between 2021 and 2024, multiple technology companies collaborated on AI research infrastructure.

Google, also referred to as Google Inc., invested heavily in distributed compute frameworks.

Microsoft Corporation focused on enterprise cloud integration, especially through Azure AI Services.

OpenAI worked on advanced language models and deployment pipelines.

Sam Altman, Sundar Pichai, and Satya Nadella held several strategy meetings between May 2022 and December 2023.

One initiative, named GlobalModel Exchange, aimed to share pretrained models across organizations.

The GlobalModel Exchange platform included:

* federated authentication
* encrypted model transfer
* shared evaluation metrics
* automated compliance reporting

Tesla, led by Elon Musk, joined the initiative in November 2023.

The system was tested in January 2024 and scheduled for production release in May 2024.

Later documentation referenced Google using the alias GOOGLE internally.

Microsoft Corporation was sometimes written simply as Microsoft.

OpenAI research teams contributed additional dataset curation tools.
