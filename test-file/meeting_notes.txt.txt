Meeting Notes — Enterprise AI Strategy

Date: July 14, 2023

Participants included:

Elon Musk from Tesla
Satya Nadella representing Microsoft
Sundar Pichai from Google
Sam Altman from OpenAI

The meeting discussed a shared research platform called UnifiedAI Hub.

UnifiedAI Hub aims to connect Tesla autonomous driving datasets with Microsoft analytics pipelines and Google AI services.

Key planned features:

* centralized training orchestration
* distributed model versioning
* autonomous dataset labeling
* secure API federation

Deployment timeline expected January 2024.

Funding discussions to continue September 2023.
